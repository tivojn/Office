## XSTYLE-01 — Consulting & Strategy

**Vibe:** McKinsey, BCG, Bain. Data-first, no-nonsense. Every element earns its place. Navy and gold authority.

- **Fonts:** Calibri (headers, bold) + Georgia (body). No decorative fonts.
- **Colors:** Deep navy `#0B1D3A` headers, `#1A202C` body text, `#64748B` muted, gold `#C9A84C` accent, `#F1F5F9` alt rows.
- **Tables:** Professional banded. Navy header row + white text. Alternating `#F1F5F9` rows. Hairline `#E2E8F0` borders. Gold accent top border on totals.
- **Charts:** Navy, gold, medium blue, slate gray series. White plot area. No gridlines. Calibri bold titles.
- **KPI Panels:** Navy `#0B1D3A` background, white `28-32pt` value, gold `10pt` label. Thick gold top accent border.
- **Conditional Formatting:** Standard green `#16A34A` / red `#DC2626`. Data bars in gold. No color scales (too noisy for consulting decks).
- **Tab Colors:** Dashboard=gold, Data=navy, Charts=blue, Reference=slate, Audit=red.
- **Design notes:** Restrained. Max 2-3 colors per sheet. No decorative images. White space is structural. Gold accent used sparingly — only on KPI panels, totals rows, and one border per section.

## XSTYLE-02 — Executive Dashboard

**Vibe:** Apple, Stripe, modern SaaS. Clean, precise, systematic. Electric blue and near-black.

- **Fonts:** Calibri (headers + body). Consistent, no-fuss. Large `36-48pt` KPI values.
- **Colors:** Near-black `#1D1D1F` headers, `#1D1D1F` body, `#6E6E73` muted, electric blue `#0071E3` accent, `#D1D1D6` borders, `#F8F9FA` alt rows.
- **Tables:** Blue header row + white text. Banded `#F8F9FA` rows. Subtle `#D1D1D6` borders. Clean card-like feel.
- **Charts:** Electric blue, gray `#6E6E73`, teal `#34C759`, amber `#FF9F0A` series. Clean. Minimal.
- **KPI Panels:** Blue `#0071E3` background, white value, light blue `#DBEAFE` label. Or: white card + blue top accent border.
- **Conditional Formatting:** Blue `#0071E3` data bars. Green `#34C759` / red `#FF3B30` status. 3-color scale for heatmaps.
- **Tab Colors:** Dashboard=blue, Data=gray, Charts=teal, Reference=slate, Audit=red.
- **Design notes:** Card-based thinking. Systematic 12pt spacing. Blue accent underlines on title rows. KPI cards as hero elements. Subtle borders — never heavy. Grid-aligned everything.

## XSTYLE-03 — Corporate Report

**Vibe:** Blue-chip traditional. Quarterly reviews, board packs. Royal blue and emerald authority.

- **Fonts:** Calibri (headers, bold) + Cambria (body). Classic pairing.
- **Colors:** Dark blue `#1E3A5F` headers, `#2D3748` body, `#718096` muted, royal blue `#2563EB` accent, emerald `#059669` accent2, `#F0F4F8` alt rows.
- **Tables:** Dark blue header + white text. Banded `#F0F4F8` rows. `#CBD5E1` borders. Emerald accents on summary rows.
- **Charts:** Royal blue, emerald, dark blue, gray series. Professional titles. Bottom legend.
- **KPI Panels:** Dark blue `#1E3A5F` background, white value, light blue `#93C5FD` label. Full box border.
- **Conditional Formatting:** Emerald `#059669` positive, red `#DC2626` negative. Royal blue data bars. Standard traffic light.
- **Tab Colors:** Dashboard=royal blue, Data=dark blue, Charts=emerald, Reference=gray, Audit=red.
- **Design notes:** Traditional structure. Clear hierarchy. Two accent colors (blue + green) — blue for primary, green for positive emphasis. Wider margins for print. Headers repeat on every printed page.

## XSTYLE-04 — Data Science & Technical

**Vibe:** Dark headers, monospace data, technical precision. IDE-inspired. Cyan, magenta, yellow accents.

- **Fonts:** Calibri (headers, bold) + Consolas (body/data). Monospace for all numeric data. Calibri for labels.
- **Colors:** Near-black `#1E1E2E` headers, `#2C2C2C` body, `#757575` muted, cyan `#00BCD4` accent, magenta `#E040FB` accent2, yellow `#FFEB3B` highlight, `#F5F5F5` alt rows.
- **Tables:** Dark `#1E1E2E` header + white text. Minimal borders `#E0E0E0`. No banding (clean grid for data). Consolas for cell data.
- **Charts:** Cyan, magenta, yellow, gray series. Dark title text. Minimal decoration. Grid-visible for precision.
- **KPI Panels:** Dark `#1E1E2E` background, cyan `28-32pt` value, `#A0A0A0` muted label. Thin cyan top accent.
- **Conditional Formatting:** Cyan positive, magenta negative. Yellow highlight for outliers. Color scale (purple→cyan gradient) for heatmaps.
- **Tab Colors:** Dashboard=cyan, Data=dark, Charts=magenta, Reference=gray, Audit=yellow.
- **Design notes:** Precision-first. Monospace for all data cells (alignment matters). Dark headers create contrast. Minimal decoration — let the data speak. Grid lines visible in data tables for readability. Code-like aesthetic.

## XSTYLE-05 — Sales & Pipeline

**Vibe:** Bold, energetic, results-driven. Coral and teal. Pipeline trackers, quota reports.

- **Fonts:** Calibri (headers + body, bold). Large `36pt` KPI values. Energetic sizing.
- **Colors:** Near-black `#0F0F1A` headers, `#1A1A2E` body, `#6B7280` muted, coral `#FF6B6B` accent, teal `#4ECDC4` accent2, yellow `#FFE66D` highlight, `#FAFAFA` alt rows.
- **Tables:** Dark `#0F0F1A` header + white text. Banded `#FAFAFA` rows. `#E5E7EB` borders. Coral accent on underperformance, teal on overperformance.
- **Charts:** Coral, teal, yellow, lavender `#9999BB` series. Bold chart titles. No gridlines.
- **KPI Panels:** Dark `#0F0F1A` background, white value, coral `#FF6B6B` label. Or: teal for positive KPIs, coral for attention-needed.
- **Conditional Formatting:** Teal `#4ECDC4` positive, coral `#FF6B6B` negative, yellow `#FFE66D` warning. Data bars in coral. Traffic light: teal/yellow/coral.
- **Tab Colors:** Dashboard=coral, Data=dark, Charts=teal, Pipeline=yellow, Audit=red.
- **Design notes:** Results-forward. Big bold numbers. Color-coded status everywhere. Teal = good, coral = attention, yellow = caution. Pipeline stages as colored progress. Dense but punchy — every cell tells a story.

## XSTYLE-06 — Finance & Accounting

**Vibe:** Conservative, traditional, audit-ready. Green and black. Budgets, forecasts, audit workpapers.

- **Fonts:** Calibri (headers, bold) + Calibri (body). Consistent, professional. Blue `#0066CC` font for input cells (industry standard).
- **Colors:** Dark forest `#1A3C2A` headers, `#2D3748` body, `#718096` muted, forest green `#2D6A4F` accent, gold `#D4A843` accent2, light green `#E8F5E9` alt rows.
- **Tables:** Forest green header + white text. Banded `#E8F5E9` rows. `#C8D6C0` borders. Bold totals with green top accent border.
- **Charts:** Forest green, gold, dark green, gray series. Conservative styling. Category labels visible.
- **KPI Panels:** Forest green `#2D6A4F` background, white value, light green label. Or: white card + green top border for lighter feel.
- **Conditional Formatting:** Green `#2D6A4F` positive, red `#DC2626` negative. Green data bars. Standard traffic light. **Industry color coding:** blue font = input, black = formula, green = link.
- **Tab Colors:** Dashboard=green, Data=dark, Assumptions=gold, P&L=forest, Balance Sheet=dark green, Audit=red.
- **Design notes:** Industry conventions strictly followed. Blue font for editable inputs. Black font for formulas. Yellow highlight for key assumptions. Light blue for input zones. Gray for locked/protected cells. Number formats explicit everywhere. Print-optimized with repeating headers.

## XSTYLE-07 — Marketing & Creative

**Vibe:** Warm, colorful, brand-forward. Terracotta and sage. Campaign reports, attribution dashboards.

- **Fonts:** Calibri (headers, bold) + Calibri (body). Warm and approachable.
- **Colors:** Dark brown `#2D1B0E` headers, `#44382C` body, `#8B7355` muted, terracotta `#C2704E` accent, sage green `#7C9A6E` accent2, cream `#FDF8F0` alt rows.
- **Tables:** Terracotta header + white text. Banded `#FDF8F0` cream rows. `#E8DDD0` borders. Sage green accents on positive metrics.
- **Charts:** Terracotta, sage, brown `#8B7355`, light terra `#D9B293` series. Warm chart titles. Cream plot area `#FFFBF5`.
- **KPI Panels:** Terracotta `#C2704E` background, white value, cream label. Or: cream card + terracotta top border.
- **Conditional Formatting:** Sage green `#7C9A6E` positive, terracotta `#C2704E` negative. Terracotta data bars. Warm color scale (cream→terracotta).
- **Tab Colors:** Dashboard=terracotta, Data=brown, Charts=sage, Campaigns=cream bg, Audit=red.
- **Design notes:** Warm tones throughout. Two-color accent system (terracotta primary + sage secondary). Cream backgrounds for a softer feel. Generous spacing. No harsh blacks — use `#2D1B0E` dark brown instead. Nature-inspired, human-centered aesthetic.

## XSTYLE-08 — Operations & Logistics

**Vibe:** Structured, clean, process-focused. Cobalt and amber. Inventory, logistics, project tracking.

- **Fonts:** Calibri (headers, bold) + Calibri (body). Clean, systematic.
- **Colors:** Charcoal `#2D3142` headers, `#2D3142` body, `#9E9E9E` muted, cobalt `#2962FF` accent, amber `#FF8F00` accent2, `#F5F5F5` alt rows.
- **Tables:** Charcoal header + white text. Banded `#F5F5F5` rows. `#E0E0E0` borders. Cobalt accent on active/in-progress items, amber on delayed/warning.
- **Charts:** Cobalt, amber, charcoal, gray `#BDBDBD` series. Clear category labels. Visible gridlines for precision.
- **KPI Panels:** Charcoal `#2D3142` background, white value, cobalt label. Status indicators: cobalt=on-track, amber=delayed.
- **Conditional Formatting:** Cobalt `#2962FF` positive/on-track, amber `#FF8F00` warning/delayed, red `#D32F2F` critical. Data bars in cobalt. Traffic light: cobalt/amber/red.
- **Tab Colors:** Dashboard=cobalt, Data=charcoal, Inventory=amber, Tracking=cobalt, Audit=red.
- **Design notes:** Process-oriented. Status-driven color coding (cobalt=good, amber=caution, red=alert). Gantt-style tracking rows. Clear column grouping for date ranges. Print-friendly with landscape orientation. Dense but structured — operations people want data, not decoration.
